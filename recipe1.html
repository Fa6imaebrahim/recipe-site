Spaghetti

Back to Home | Back to Italian Cuisine

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation Time

30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta with garlic in olive oil until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with parmesan and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and then mix in egg mixture.
  5. Serve with extra parmesan and black pepper.
