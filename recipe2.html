Margherita Pizza

Back to Home | Back to Italian Cuisine

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Stagioni Pizza Napoletana Flour
  • 600 ml Water
  • 2 grams Fresh yeast
  • 30 grams Sea salt
  • 1 tin San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Handful Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 100 grams Mozzarella
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Preparation Time

minutes

Instructions

  1. Dissolve 20g salt in 600ml water.Add 100g flour and mix.Add yeast, then gradually mix in the rest of the flour.
  2. Rest for 18 hrs, then shape into 270g balls and prove for 4-6 hrs.
  3. Stretch dough on a floured surface, leaving a 1cm rim. Flip and stretch into a circle.
  4. Crush San Marzano tomatoes with salt and basil. Spread on dough, top with mozzarella, Pecorino, basil, and olive oil.
  5. Bake in a 450Â°C woodfire oven for 90 seconds, turning halfway.
  6. Enjoy!
