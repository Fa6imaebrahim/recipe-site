Chicken Shawarma

Back to Home | Back to Arabic Cuisine

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken thighs (boneless and skinless)
  • 4 Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 tsp Ground cumin
  • 2 tsp Ground coriander
  • 1 tsp Ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp Ground paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Ground cloves
  • 1/2 cup Plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread or flatbread (for serving)
  • Garlic sauce (for serving)

Preparation Time

40 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the olive oil, garlic, cumin, coriander, turmeric, paprika, cinnamon, cloves, yogurt, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cut the chicken thighs into strips or bite-sized pieces and marinate them in the yogurt mixture. Let it sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked and charred slightly.
  4. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pan and set it aside for a few minutes.
  5. Warm pita bread or flatbread, then place the chicken on the bread and drizzle with garlic sauce, if desired.
  6. Wrap the shawarma in the bread and serve hot!
